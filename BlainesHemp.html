What is Blaine's Hemp Protein?

Blaine's Hemp Protein is just one of many revolutionary products by Blaine's Hemp Products that's main focus is on sustainability and beneficial products for the consumer. Hemp seeds contain plenty of protein, healthful fatty acids, and fiber. Hemp seeds are also a healthful source of vitamin E and minerals, such as calcium, iron, magnesium, potassium, and zinc. Blaine's Hemp Protein takes all of these benefits and combines it into a proprietary blend to deliver a superior protein product to meet your goals in the gym and all active walks of life.
